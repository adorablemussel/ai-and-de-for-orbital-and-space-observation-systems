CAM METHODS COMPARISON
======================
- Do all CAM methods highlight the same image regions? 
  Generally yes, but with varying degrees of localization and boundaries.
- Which method produces the sharpest visualization? 
  EigenCAM (strictly isolates high-contrast boundaries).
- Which method produces the smoothest visualization? 
  HiResCAM / GradCAM.
- Which method appears easiest to interpret? 
  GradCAM and LayerCAM (clear gradient mapping over key objects).
- Do any methods produce suspicious or unexpected attention? 
  Yes, EigenCAM completely ignores the central infrastructure and shifts to the right boundary.
- What does this suggest about relying on only one XAI method? 
  Single methods can mislead; ensemble cross-verification is mandatory for robust deployment.
