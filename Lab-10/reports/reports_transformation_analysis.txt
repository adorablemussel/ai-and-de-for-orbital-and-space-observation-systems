TRANSFORMATION ANALYSIS
=======================
- Did confidence change? 
  Negligible change for flips/rotations; severe drops occurred under Gaussian noise and blur.
- Did attention regions move? 
  They rotated/flipped symmetrically with valid geometric transforms, but became chaotic under noise.
- Which transformations had the largest impact? 
  Gaussian noise and heavy blur.
- Which transformations had minimal impact? 
  Horizontal flips and 90-degree rotations.
- Does the model appear robust? 
  Robust to spatial adjustments, but highly fragile against pixel-level corruptions and high-frequency atmospheric noise.
