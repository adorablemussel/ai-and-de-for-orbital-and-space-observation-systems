CLASS ATTENTION CONSISTENCY
===========================
- Does the model consistently focus on similar regions? 
  Consistent for residential (buildings) and forest (textures), but highly inconsistent for rivers.
- Does attention vary significantly? 
  Yes, for rivers, the focus shifts entirely based on surrounding background infrastructure.
- Which class produces the most stable attention patterns? 
  Residential (highly stable geometric features).
- Which class appears more difficult? 
  River (the model avoids the water medium and relies completely on shore context).
