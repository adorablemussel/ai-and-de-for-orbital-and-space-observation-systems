OCCLUSION ANALYSIS
==================
- Do both methods highlight similar regions? 
  Yes, both target the shore infrastructure intersection rather than the river itself.
- Which explanation appears more localized? 
  Occlusion sensitivity (bounded strictly by the sliding patch size).
- Which explanation appears easier to interpret? 
  Grad-CAM (smooth, continuous gradients are visually intuitive).
- Did both methods identify the river region? 
  No, both highlighted the non-water background elements on the shore.
- What happens for unknown inputs? 
  Occlusion yields scattered, noisy, or uniformly low sensitivity values.
- Can one explanation method completely replace another? 
  No. Grad-CAM (gradient-based) and Occlusion (perturbation-based) complement each other by validating different behavioral properties.
