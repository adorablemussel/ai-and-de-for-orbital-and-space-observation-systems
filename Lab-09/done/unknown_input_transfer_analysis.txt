Does the transfer model also force a known class prediction?
Yes. The Softmax activation still forces the output probabilities to sum to 1.0 across the known classes.

Does it give high confidence on unknown input?
Yes. Deep neural networks, including transfer learning models, suffer from overconfidence and often assign >90% probability to random or unknown images.

Is transfer learning enough to solve out-of-distribution detection?
No. Transfer learning only improves feature representation; it does not change the closed-set nature of the classifier.

What additional mechanism would be needed in an operational system?
An Out-of-Distribution (OOD) detection module, such as uncertainty estimation (entropy), feature-space distance analysis, or an explicit "Unknown/Anomaly" class.
