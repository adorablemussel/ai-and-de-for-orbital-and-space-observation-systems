FINAL ENGINEERING DECISION
==========================

Selected model: 
ResNet18 with frozen feature extractor.

Technical justification:
This model provided the best trade-off between accuracy, training time, and implementation complexity. Leveraging ImageNet features allowed it to achieve high accuracy (~90%) despite the very small dataset (240 training images).

Expected advantages:
1. High accuracy compared to training from scratch.
2. Fast and stable training (only the final fully connected layer was updated).
3. Strong resistance to overfitting due to frozen convolutional layers.

Possible risks:
1. The model assumes a closed-set environment and will confidently misclassify Out-of-Distribution (OOD) images (e.g., clouds, sensor noise).
2. The dataset might be too small to cover all intra-class variations (e.g., seasonal changes in forests).

Deployment considerations:
While excellent for prototyping, a real operational deployment requires implementing OOD detection mechanisms and proper input normalization to ensure the model doesn't silently fail when encountering unexpected data.
